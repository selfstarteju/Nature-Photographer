My Photography Journey

From a curious beginner to a passionate nature lover

🌄

How It Started

My love for nature photography began during a family trip to the Western Ghats. The misty mountains and lush green valleys completely mesmerized me. I picked up my phone camera and started capturing everything I could see!

📱

Learning & Growing

Starting with just my smartphone, I learned about composition, lighting, and timing. Every weekend became an adventure - exploring local parks, hills, and hidden spots around Maharashtra to practice my skills.

❤️

My Passion Today

Now, photography isn't just a hobby - it's my way of connecting with nature. Each photo tells a story of patience, wonder, and the incredible beauty that surrounds us every day. I hope my photos inspire others to love nature too!
